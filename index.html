12 Years of Crafting Happiness
Our Heritage

Crafting Perfect Cups & Rich Memories

Founded in 2014, **CAFÉ AROMA** was born out of a simple passion: to brew the ultimate cup of coffee and create a warm sanctuary where people gather, spark ideas, and share meaningful conversations.

We source our single-origin, 100% Organic Arabica beans directly from the high-altitude estate farms of Chikmagalur. Every batch is roasted under precision-controlled temperatures by our master roasters, ensuring the complex floral, fruity, and chocolate notes are preserved.
